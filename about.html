About Power Smart Australia

We help Australian families understand how their appliances use energy and save money on electricity bills.

What We Do

Power Smart Australia provides simple information about appliance energy use. We want to help Australian households reduce their electricity bills and help the environment.

With electricity prices rising in Australia, it's important to understand which appliances use the most energy. Our website gives you practical tips to save money.
